About

Luis Ramirez is an American Front-End Web Developer. He now resides in Los Angeles, creating websites for clients and work, as well as pursuing his own web projects. He was raised by immigrant parents in South Central Los Angeles, which was a notoriously dangerous region in Los Angeles known for its extensive history of gang violence. Then he studied Environmental Studies at the University of California, Santa Barbara. He enjoys playing video games, mixed-martial-arts, traveling and reading.
